About us


The 1.1 million strong Indian Army lost one braveheart every 3 days in the last 13 years. After years of hardwork and moments of great courage, they face their end, leaving behind their families after serving the nation selflessly.

Left without their breadwinners, families after families have to wait around for government funds and handouts which may take a long time to be distributed. This is why we created Tribute.

Tribute is an online funding website created with the aim of providing financial support to the families left behind by martyred soldiers. So many common people want to help such families of martyred soldiers, but don't know how. We make sure that those who want to help, can do so securely and quite easily, so we created this

The funds go directly to the bank accounts of an authenticated family member, and we make sure to provide a certificate to each donater!

It's not about how much we give , but how much love we put into giving, so if you wish to, and if you can, consider making a small donation for this very noble cause.
